Wood Property Group

is a privately owned property & investment company primarily operating across Scotland.

Our key focus is commercial & industrial property investment, site development and unique land ventures for commercialisation projects.

With an experienced team along with our retained professional advisors we take a proactive entrepreneurial approach to commercial property investment creating enhanced revenue streams, income and yield, our ability to deliver profitable commercialisation across our projects is testimony to our experience and expertise in the property sector.

We also collaborate & JV with developers, land owners & investors on larger projects and open to new investment and commercial development opportunities.

Commercial Property Investment

Our key focus is to grow our commercial and land portfolio across Scotland with freehold acquisition of commercial, industrial and suitable land for commercialisation. We also consider distressed commercial properties or sites requiring investment.
